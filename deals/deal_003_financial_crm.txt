DEAL POST-MORTEM: Financial Services CRM - Global Finance Corp
==============================================================

Deal: Global Finance Corp - Enterprise CRM Platform
Sales Rep: Jennifer Martinez
Industry: Financial Services
Deal Value: $650,000 ARR
Closed: May 15, 2024
Status: Lost to Competitor

CHRONOLOGICAL EVENT TIMELINE
-----------------------------

Day 1 (February 5, 2024) - Initial Contact
- Warm introduction through industry conference networking
- Initial discovery call with Head of Sales Operations
- Discussed current CRM limitations and pain points
- Identified need for better lead management and reporting
- Sent initial proposal and demo access
- Sentiment: Very Positive
- Notes: Strong chemistry, immediate interest expressed

Day 10 (February 14, 2024) - Product Demonstration
- Comprehensive product demonstration to 10 stakeholders
- Attendees: Sales, Marketing, Operations, IT teams
- Demo went very well, received positive feedback
- Customer impressed with advanced reporting capabilities
- Requested custom demo for their specific workflows
- Sentiment: Positive
- Notes: High engagement, multiple stakeholders interested

Day 20 (February 24, 2024) - Custom Demo Delivery
- Customized demo built specifically for their workflows
- Showed integration with Salesforce (they use SFDC currently)
- Demonstrated advanced reporting and analytics features
- Technical team very impressed with capabilities
- Sentiment: Positive
- Notes: Technical validation successful

Day 30 (March 5, 2024) - Pricing Discussion Begins
- Initial pricing: $650,000 ARR
- Customer budget: $500,000 ARR
- Gap identified: $150,000 (23% difference)
- Customer requested discount
- Proposed $600,000 ARR
- Sentiment: Neutral
- Notes: Pricing negotiation started

Day 40 (March 15, 2024) - Integration Concerns Raised
- Technical integration discussion
- Customer concerned about Salesforce integration complexity
- Requested dedicated integration support team
- We offered standard support, not dedicated team
- Integration support became major concern
- Sentiment: Negative
- Notes: Integration support gap identified

Day 50 (March 25, 2024) - Competitor Enters Picture
- Customer mentioned evaluating alternative solution
- Competitor: SalesForce Pro (different from their current SFDC)
- Competitor offering better native Salesforce integration
- Customer requested detailed comparison matrix
- Sentiment: Negative
- Notes: Competitive threat emerged

Day 60 (April 4, 2024) - Best and Final Offer Requested
- Customer requested best and final offer
- Submitted: $550,000 ARR with dedicated integration support
- Customer said still evaluating both options
- Sentiment: Neutral
- Notes: Final proposal submitted

Day 70 (April 14, 2024) - Decision Delay
- Follow-up call scheduled but postponed twice by customer
- Finally connected after 5 days
- Customer said decision delayed due to internal reorganization
- Mentioned new stakeholders need to review
- Sentiment: Neutral
- Notes: Decision process unclear

Day 80 (April 24, 2024) - Final Follow-up Attempt
- Final email sent requesting decision timeline
- No response for 8 days
- Called, left voicemail
- No callback received
- Sentiment: Negative
- Notes: Communication breakdown

May 15, 2024 - Lost Notification Received
- Received email: Deal lost to SalesForce Pro
- Reasons provided:
  1. Better native Salesforce integration (built specifically for SF ecosystem)
  2. Lower price ($520,000 vs $550,000)
  3. Dedicated integration team included at base price
  4. Faster implementation timeline (2 months vs 3 months)
  5. Better fit for Salesforce-centric organization
- Sentiment: Very Negative
- Outcome: Closed Lost

ROOT CAUSES
-----------
1. Integration support: Competitor offered dedicated team, we offered standard support
2. Price: 6% higher than competitor ($550K vs $520K)
3. Timeline: 3 months vs competitor's 2 months
4. Native integration: Competitor had native Salesforce integration, we had API-based
5. Communication: Lost momentum in final weeks with delayed responses

COMPETITIVE INTELLIGENCE
------------------------
Competitor: SalesForce Pro
- Pricing: $520,000 ARR (6% lower)
- Integration: Native Salesforce integration (not API-based)
- Support: Dedicated integration team included
- Timeline: 2 months implementation
- Advantage: Built specifically for Salesforce ecosystem
- Our disadvantage: API-based integration more complex

MEETING NOTES
-------------
- Total meetings: 8 (discovery, demo x2, pricing x2, integration, best/final, follow-up)
- Key stakeholders: Head of Sales Ops, CTO, VP Sales, Procurement
- Decision makers: CTO and VP Sales (limited engagement after initial meetings)

NEGOTIATION TIMELINE
--------------------
- Initial quote: $650,000 ARR (Mar 5)
- First counter: $600,000 ARR (Mar 5)
- Second counter: $550,000 ARR with dedicated support (Apr 4)
- Customer budget: $500,000 ARR
- Competitor: $520,000 ARR
- Final gap: $30,000 (6%) but customer chose competitor for integration benefits

DELIVERY PLANNING
-----------------
- Proposed timeline: 3 months implementation
- Customer requirement: 2 months preferred
- Gap: 1 month
- Competitor offered 2-month guaranteed timeline
- We could not guarantee 2 months without risk

COMMUNICATION QUALITY
---------------------
- Initial: Excellent (warm intro helped)
- Mid-process: Good, regular follow-ups
- Final weeks: Poor (delayed responses, communication breakdown)
- Written confirmations: Some integration discussions were verbal only
