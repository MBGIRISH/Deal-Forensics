LOST DEAL ANALYSIS: Retail Analytics Platform - MegaRetail Corp
=================================================================

Deal: MegaRetail Corp - Advanced Analytics Solution
Owner: David Kim
Industry: Retail
Value: $240,000 ARR
Close Date: June 8, 2024
Stage: Closed Lost

EVENT CHRONOLOGY
----------------

March 5, 2024 - Initial Discovery Call
- Inbound lead from website contact form
- Discovery call with VP of Analytics
- Discussed current analytics challenges and limitations
- Identified need for real-time dashboards and predictive analytics
- Sent product information and case studies
- Sentiment: Positive
- Outcome: Strong initial interest

March 12, 2024 - Technical Evaluation Meeting
- Technical evaluation meeting with analytics team
- Demonstrated analytics capabilities and real-time features
- Customer very interested in predictive analytics features
- Requested proof of concept (POC) with their sample data
- Agreed to 2-week POC period
- Sentiment: Positive
- Outcome: POC approved

March 19, 2024 - POC Kickoff
- POC started with customer's sample retail dataset
- Initial results very positive
- Customer satisfied with predictive analytics accuracy
- Customer requested additional features during POC
- POC scope expanded beyond original agreement
- Sentiment: Neutral
- Outcome: POC progressing but scope expanding

March 26, 2024 - POC Results Review
- POC completed successfully
- Customer very satisfied with results
- Requested pricing proposal
- Sent initial quote: $240,000 ARR
- Customer budget: $180,000 ARR
- Sentiment: Negative (pricing gap)
- Outcome: Significant pricing gap identified

April 2, 2024 - Pricing Negotiation Round 1
- Negotiated pricing down to $210,000 ARR
- Customer still said too high
- Requested $190,000 ARR
- We couldn't go below $200,000 ARR
- Pricing stalemate
- Sentiment: Negative
- Outcome: Pricing negotiation stalled

April 9, 2024 - Feature Requests Discussion
- Customer requested additional features not in base package
- Custom reporting module requested
- Advanced data visualization features requested
- We quoted additional $35,000 for custom features
- Customer said not in budget
- Sentiment: Negative
- Outcome: Feature requirements vs budget conflict

April 16, 2024 - Competitor Mentioned
- Customer evaluating alternative vendor
- Competitor: DataViz Pro Analytics
- Competitor offering lower price and included requested features
- Customer requested best and final offer
- Submitted final: $200,000 ARR with standard features
- Sentiment: Negative
- Outcome: Competitive pressure high

April 23, 2024 - Decision Delay
- Customer said need time to evaluate both options
- Mentioned budget approval process taking longer
- Follow-up scheduled for early May
- Sentiment: Neutral
- Outcome: Decision delayed

May 7, 2024 - Final Follow-up
- Follow-up call made
- Customer said still evaluating both solutions
- No clear timeline provided
- Sentiment: Neutral
- Outcome: Decision still pending

June 8, 2024 - Deal Lost
- Received notification: Lost to DataViz Pro Analytics
- Reasons: Lower price ($185,000 vs $200,000), included requested features at base price
- Sentiment: Negative
- Outcome: Closed Lost

BLOCKERS
--------
1. Pricing gap: $20,000 final difference (10%)
2. Feature requirements: Custom features not in budget
3. Competitive pressure: Competitor had better value proposition
4. Budget constraints: Strict $180K budget limit

COMPETITOR
----------
DataViz Pro Analytics
- Pricing: $185,000 ARR (8% lower)
- Features: Included custom reporting at base price
- Advantage: Better value for requested features
