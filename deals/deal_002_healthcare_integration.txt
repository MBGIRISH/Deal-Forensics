DEAL FORENSICS: Healthcare System Integration - MedHealth Group
================================================================

Deal Name: MedHealth Group - Healthcare System Integration Platform
Owner: Michael Chen
Industry: Healthcare
Value: $380,000
Close Date: April 18, 2024
Stage: Closed Lost

TIMELINE OF EVENTS
------------------

Week 1 (January 10, 2024) - Initial Discovery
- Cold outreach via email to Director of IT
- Response received within 3 days
- Initial discovery call scheduled
- Discussed current system limitations and integration needs
- Identified 20+ integration points needed with Epic and Cerner systems
- Sent initial proposal and technical overview
- Sentiment: Positive
- Notes: Strong initial engagement, clear requirements

Week 2 (January 17, 2024) - Requirements Gathering Session
- Requirements gathering session with IT team (6 people)
- Detailed discussion of integration requirements
- Identified compliance requirements (HIPAA, SOC 2)
- Security and compliance became key concerns
- Requested detailed security documentation and compliance certifications
- Sentiment: Neutral
- Notes: Requirements more complex than initially thought

Week 3 (January 24, 2024) - Technical Evaluation
- Technical deep dive with integration team
- Demonstrated API capabilities and integration architecture
- Discussed data mapping requirements for 20+ integration points
- Customer raised concerns about API rate limits
- Requested custom API endpoints for high-volume transactions
- Sentiment: Neutral to Negative
- Notes: Technical requirements expanding beyond standard offering

Week 4 (January 31, 2024) - Compliance Review
- Security and compliance review meeting
- Provided HIPAA compliance documentation
- Provided SOC 2 Type I certification
- Customer requested SOC 2 Type II certification (we have Type I, Type II in progress)
- Compliance gap identified
- Sentiment: Negative
- Notes: Compliance certification gap became concern

Week 5 (February 7, 2024) - Pricing Discussion
- Initial quote: $380,000 one-time + $60,000 annual maintenance
- Customer budget: $280,000 total (one-time + first year maintenance)
- Significant gap identified: $100,000 (26% difference)
- Customer requested payment plan options
- Proposed $350,000 with extended payment terms (3 years)
- Sentiment: Negative
- Notes: Budget constraints major issue

Week 6 (February 14, 2024) - Pricing Negotiation
- Negotiated to $320,000 with 3-year payment plan
- Customer still said too high, requested $300,000
- We proposed $310,000 as final offer
- Customer said need to evaluate alternatives
- Sentiment: Negative
- Notes: Pricing negotiation stalled

Week 7 (February 21, 2024) - Competitor Activity
- Customer mentioned evaluating alternative vendor
- Competitor: HealthTech Integration Solutions
- Competitor offering lower price and faster implementation
- Customer requested best and final offer
- Submitted revised proposal: $300,000 with 3-year terms
- Sentiment: Very Negative
- Notes: Competitive pressure mounting

Week 8 (February 28, 2024) - Delivery Planning Discussion
- Discussed implementation approach and timeline
- Proposed 8-month phased rollout
- Customer needed 5-month aggressive timeline
- Resource allocation concerns raised
- Delivery timeline became major blocker
- Sentiment: Negative
- Notes: Timeline mismatch significant

Week 9 (March 7, 2024) - Executive Escalation
- Escalated to VP of Operations and CFO
- Presented business case and ROI analysis
- Executive interested but concerned about timeline and compliance
- Requested additional healthcare-specific references
- Sentiment: Neutral
- Notes: Executive engagement limited

Week 10 (March 14, 2024) - Follow-up and Decision Delay
- Follow-up email sent
- No response for 7 days
- Called to check status
- Customer said decision delayed due to budget review and compliance audit
- Sentiment: Neutral
- Notes: Decision process stalled

Week 11 (March 21, 2024) - Final Communication
- Final follow-up call
- Customer indicated leaning toward competitor
- Primary reasons: lower cost, faster timeline, better compliance certifications
- Asked if we could match competitor terms
- We could not match without significant risk
- Sentiment: Negative
- Notes: Deal at high risk

April 18, 2024 - Final Outcome - Deal Lost
- Received formal notification: Deal lost to HealthTech Integration Solutions
- Reasons provided:
  1. Lower total cost ($280,000 vs $300,000)
  2. Faster implementation (5 months vs 8 months)
  3. SOC 2 Type II certification available immediately
  4. More healthcare-specific references and case studies
  5. Better integration expertise for Epic/Cerner systems
- Sentiment: Very Negative
- Outcome: Closed Lost

BLOCKERS SUMMARY
----------------
1. Budget gap: $20,000 final difference (7%)
2. Timeline: Customer needed 5 months, we proposed 8 months minimum
3. Compliance: SOC 2 Type II certification gap
4. Competitive pressure: HealthTech had better healthcare credentials
5. Resource constraints: Customer needed dedicated team, we couldn't guarantee
6. Integration complexity: 20+ integration points more complex than standard

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS
--------------------
Competitor: HealthTech Integration Solutions
- Pricing: $280,000 total (7% lower)
- Timeline: 5 months (faster)
- Compliance: SOC 2 Type II certified immediately
- Healthcare focus: Stronger vertical expertise
- References: More healthcare-specific case studies
- Integration: Specialized Epic/Cerner integration team

COMMUNICATION ANALYSIS
----------------------
- Initial communication: Excellent (quick response)
- Mid-process: Good, regular follow-ups
- Final weeks: Communication broke down (7-day response delay)
- Response times: Generally good until final weeks
- Written confirmations: Some pricing discussions were verbal only
- Executive engagement: Limited after escalation

MEETING LOGS
------------
- Total meetings: 10 (discovery, requirements, technical x2, compliance, pricing x2, delivery, escalation, follow-up)
- Key stakeholders: IT Director, VP Operations, CFO, Integration Team
- Decision makers: CFO and VP Operations (limited direct engagement)

NEGOTIATION DETAILS
-------------------
- Initial quote: $380,000 + $60K maintenance = $440,000 total
- First counter: $350,000 with 3-year terms
- Second counter: $320,000 with 3-year terms
- Final offer: $300,000 with 3-year terms
- Customer budget: $280,000 total
- Competitor: $280,000 total
- Final gap: $20,000 (7%) but customer chose competitor

DELIVERY TIMELINE ISSUES
-------------------------

- Customer needed: 5 months
- Gap: 3 months
- Customer requested guaranteed timeline
- We could not guarantee 5 months without risk
- Competitor offered 5-month guarantee

ESCALATION POINTS
-----------------
- Compliance certification gap (Week 4)
- Pricing negotiation stalled (Week 6)
- Competitor activity (Week 7)
- Delivery timeline concerns (Week 8)
- Executive escalation (Week 9)
- Final decision delay (Week 10)

FINAL DECISION
--------------
April 18, 2024 - Lost to HealthTech Integration Solutions
